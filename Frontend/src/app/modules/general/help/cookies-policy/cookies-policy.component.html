1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They serve various purposes, such as helping the app remember your preferences or analyzing how the app is used to improve user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies:

These cookies are necessary for the basic functions of Arrange. They enable core functionalities such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies through the app settings.

Analytics Cookies:

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with Arrange. This helps us improve our services and enhance your experience. These cookies collect information in an anonymous form.

Advertising Cookies:

Advertising cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an ad and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences directly through the app settings. Here, you can choose to accept or decline specific types of cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of Arrange.
