Welcome to Arrange, where your account security and control are our top priorities. I'm Dev Patel, and I'm thrilled to introduce you to our comprehensive "Manage Account" feature. At Arrange, we understand the importance of keeping your personal information safe and accessible. Here, you can update your profile details, manage passwords with our robust security measures, and tailor privacy settings to your preferences. Whether it's changing passwords, updating contact information, or setting up two-factor authentication, Arrange empowers you with intuitive tools to safeguard your account effectively. Explore our features and take control of your account security today.

Profile Information

Update Profile Information

• Users can change their profile picture, display name, and other personal details.

• Changes are confirmed using the account password.

• Confirmation email is sent to the registered email address for any profile updates.

Password Management

Change Password

• Users can update their account password with a maximum length of 40 characters, allowing any characters.

• Password strength meter is provided to help users create strong passwords.

• Password changes require confirmation via email to the registered email address.

Forgot Password

• Users can request a password reset via email.

• Email contains a secure link to a password reset page where a new password can be set.

Email Management

Change Email Address

• Users can request to change their email address.

• A confirmation email is sent to the new email address with a secure link to verify and complete the change.

• The process includes setting a new password for security.

Phone Number Management

Update Phone Number

• Users can add or update their phone number.

• SMS verification is required to confirm and save the new phone number.

• Users receive a verification code via SMS to complete the process.

Privacy Settings

Profile Visibility

• Users have full control over who can view their profile information (e.g., public, friends-only).

• Settings are adjustable under privacy preferences.

Security Notifications

Notification Preferences

• Users can choose their preferred communication method (email or SMS) for receiving security alerts.

• Notifications are sent for suspicious login attempts, profile updates, and other critical account activities.
